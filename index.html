Gem Collector

Playing the game

Use the arrow keys to move your player up, down, left, and right respectively. The object of the game is to collect all of the gems while making it across the stone path to the water without getting hit by enemy bugs.

Collecting Gems

Land on the square with a gem in it to collect the gem and to get bonus points.

Level up

Complete each level by reaching the water. This will level you up and increase your points.

  • Collecting a gem is worth 20 points
  • Completing a level is worth 100 points

Game over

If you get hit by an enemy bug the game is over and it will automatically reset so that you can play again.
